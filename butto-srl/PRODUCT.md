# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary B2B: Municipal administrators, institutional procurement officers, facilities managers at schools/hospitals/condominiums in Sicily (EN province). Context: evaluating suppliers for public tender contracts — need verifiable credentials, documented track record, legal compliance. Job: find a certified, reliable environmental services partner they can defend choosing.

Secondary B2C: Private individuals and small business owners needing one-off or recurring services (pest control, premises clearance, hazardous waste disposal). Context: searching for a trustworthy local provider. Job: quickly assess credibility and make contact.

## Product Purpose

Buttò S.r.l. (founded 1978, Assoro EN) delivers integrated environmental services across Sicily: certified waste management, institutional sanitation (schools, hospitals, government offices), urban green maintenance, and circular economy consulting. The site is a lead generation surface dressed as a brand statement — it converts institutional trust into contact requests.

## Brand Personality

Autorevole. Trasparente. Rassicurante.

Physical-object reference: a certified environmental laboratory report, a government waste-tracking manifest, a precision calibration sheet. Authority through process visibility, not rhetoric. A procurement officer reading this site should think: "these people know exactly what they're doing and I can defend this choice."

## Anti-references

- Generic eco-corporate: bright green + stock leaf photos + round sans-serif + mission-statement hero. Invisible in the sector; associates with NGO greenwashing, not industrial operators.
- SaaS startup white: white cards, purple/blue accents, metric hero template, testimonial carousel. Wrong register entirely — B2B services, not software.
- Government institutional dull: tabular layouts, no visual identity, burocrazia not competence.
- Greenwashing-superficial: pastels, rounded corners, tree/leaf iconography as wallpaper, "saving the planet" copy. Undermines authoritative industrial operator positioning.
- Italian Editorial Industrial lane (current design's forest/ochre/Cormorant): beautiful but wrong for the corporate-eco professional pivot.

## Design Principles

1. **Authority through transparency**: show the process, the certifications, the numbers. Demonstrate credibility with specifics, never assert it.
2. **Precision over decoration**: every visual element earns its place by communicating competence. Nothing purely ornamental.
3. **Scale communicates trustworthiness**: generous white space and confident proportions signal an organization that doesn't need to oversell.
4. **Ecology is the system, not the slogan**: green appears as structure and careful accent, not as iconographic leaf-and-earth wallpaper.
5. **Scroll as discovery**: scroll engineering reveals new information rather than replaying the same entrance animation on every section.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 on all backgrounds. Reduced-motion support mandatory — every animation needs a `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` equivalent. Body font minimum 16px on mobile. Form labels always visible (no placeholder-only fields).
